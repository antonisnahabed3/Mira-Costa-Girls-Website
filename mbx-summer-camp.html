MBX Summer Camp

The MBX Summer Camp is what MCHS athletes use to train during the summer. It is important for the athletes in our program to continue building on their skills and working together as a team throughout the year, so we can become the best we can be collectively while staying competitive in our league and CIF.

For the 26/27 season, all girls, including incoming freshmen, will have training from 6-8am Tuesday/Thursday from June 15-July 17.

You will register through MBX.
